## About Mood Journal

**Mood Journal** is a web application that helps you track, reflect, and understand your emotions daily.  
Through simple entries and mood analytics, you can gain insights into your emotional well-being over time.  

Key features include:

- **Daily Mood Tracking** – Quickly log how you feel each day.  
- **Personal Journaling** – Add thoughts, experiences, and highlights to your entries.  
- **Mood Analytics** – Visualize trends and patterns in your emotions over weeks and months.  
- **Private & Secure** – Your data is personal and safely protected.  

Built with **Laravel** and modern web technologies, Mood Journal is accessible, responsive, and designed to make daily reflection effortless.
